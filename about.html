Why Kerala needs AI literacy

Kerala has a history of digital literacy and can become a global model for a human-centred AI literacy program. The initiative seeks to make knowledge a public good and empower citizens to create responsibly with AI.

The Challenge

  • Students graduating without AI knowledge and future-ready skills.
  • Workforce transitions: automation impacts jobs in service industries.
  • Misinformation & deepfakes — elders are particularly vulnerable.

Our aim is to reach learners across Kerala and ensure communities can both understand and build AI-led solutions.

Local examples where AI helps

Education

AI tutors and multilingual learning aids for schools and libraries.

Agriculture

Simple crop-disease classifiers and advisory systems for small holders.

Disaster preparedness

Alert systems that use AI to aggregate weather and local sensors.
