Our Approach

We teach with the head, hand and heart model — understanding, building and sharing.

Head

Explain how AI works: pattern recognition, fairness, limitations, and ethics.

Hand

Tools and hands-on projects: Teachable Machine, small LLM demos, dataset creation.

Heart

Encourage mentoring, community sharing and local language content to make AI inclusive.

Target groups

  • KuttyMakers (ages 10–17) — pattern games and storybooks
  • Young Makers (students & early professionals) — hackathons, projects
  • Friends of the Movement — educators, professionals, community leaders
